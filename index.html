Domo's 2022 tour

Tour stop one: Kansas City, MO

Kansas city is the easiest, quickest trip to take besides chicago. its three hours, straight shot down 70 east. While in kansas city i stopped by a hookah bar for great vibes, drinks, and a fun time. For eating, i went to this concert to see my friend perform and thats always a good time as well!

Tour stop two: Houston, Texas!!

Houston was by far one of the most fun trips I've been on. Houston is filled with nothing but vibes, fun, things to do, places to see. Despite it being cold, I had the time of my life. I can't wait to visit again and get to experience more of what Texas has to offer!!
